My Friends Cricle


Living together at Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Pokhal in Tehri Garhwal, Uttarakhand, for seven years has forged a bond of friendship that goes beyond the ordinary. The hostel life has been a canvas where countless memories have been painted with laughter, shared dreams, and mutual support. Late-night conversations, shared meals, and studying together have created a deep sense of camaraderie. Through the ups and downs, the close-knit group of friends has become like a family, supporting each other in every endeavor and creating a treasure trove of unforgettable moments. This unique blend of friendship and togetherness is a testament to the strength of the bonds formed in the shared environment of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya.
